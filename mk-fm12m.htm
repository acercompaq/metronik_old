Metallic muffle furnace

MK-FM12M

 

It makes it possible to execute thermal treatments in high-hydrogen content atmosphere, in relative safety.

The metallic muffle furnace is expressly designed to carry out sintering and brazing treatments in the fields of diamond abrasive tools, and composite powders and materials.

The quality of products sintered in a furnace in a reactive atmosphere is better than that of products sintered in traditional furnaces.

 

Dimensions             

Width: 520 mm.

Depth: 650 mm.

Height: 650 mm.

Weight: 60 Kg.

           

Technical specifications

Usable width: 180 mm.

Usable height: 130mm.

Usable depth: 400 mm.

Working temperature: 1200° C.

Heating by Kanthal resistors.

Installed power: 4 kW.

Monophase power supply:220V, 50Hz.

Isolation in ceramic fibre.

 

The furnace has a chimney with a burner for the combustion of exhaust gases.

Temperature control is ensured by a thermocouple electronic thermoregulator.

A particular safety system prevents the operator from performing dangerous operations.

 

Options

Electronic microprocessor programmer for thermal treatment cycle.

Support trailer for the pieces dumped from the furnace.

MK-FM12M

 

Equipo que permite efectuar tratamientos térmicos en atmósfera con alto contenido de hidrógeno, en buenas condiciones de seguridad.

El horno fue estudiado expresamente para conseguir tratamientos de sinterizción y quemado, en el campo de los abrasivos diamantados, de los polvos y de los materiales compuestos.

La calidad del producto sinterizado en un horno de atmósfera reactiva es sin dudas superior a otros métodos.
