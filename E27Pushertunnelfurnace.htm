Pusher tunnel furnace MK-SC2000

Technical specifications

Maximum temperature:1200° C.

Absorbed power: 30 kW.

Kanthal resistors wrapped around aluminium silicate pipes.

Isolation in rigid 1260 ceramic fibre slabs.

Refractory steel muffle.

Muffle width:150 mm.

Muffle height: 100 mm.

Total muffle length: 3950 mm.

Heating part length: 2000 mm.

Cooling entry zone length: 500 mm.

Thrust and extraction cylinder stroke: 450 mm.

The heating zone has two separate groups of resistors which allow for two different temperature

regulations; regulation units on both entry and exit sides.

The temperature is controlled by two electrical thermocouple regulators; the resistors are stoked

by impulse train SCR.

A timer determines the introduction and extraction rate in full temperature of the treated materials.

The thrust group allows for the insertion of pieces to be treated in the electrical tunnel furnace.

An extraction group makes it possible to remove the pieces from the electrical tunnel furnace.

The entry-exit side doors are supplied with ceramic fibre puddening and a nitrogen barrier.

Two pipes with burner flames make it possible to burn the exhaust gases from the muffle.

A particular safety system prevents the operator from performing dangerous operations.
