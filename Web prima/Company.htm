METRONIK snc – a leading company in the field of industrial machinery – has, in the course of its twenty-five years of activity, specialized in the designing and production of machines  for the abrasive tool sector and now boats among its customer some of the most poriminent Italian and foreing companies in the field.

 

Thanks to its know-how and its long years of experience, METRONIK snc does not simply produce machinary but can provide its customers with the know-how needed to produce abrasive tools.

  

METRONIK snc, has therefore developed specific installations for the production of different kinds of abrasives, including:

 

          - Metallic diamond abrasive

          - Magnesite abrasives and silicon carbide or corundum chip

          - Abrasives with reseines and glass-bonded silicon carbide or corundum chips

          - Pressed abrasives with diamond or tetraboron carbide with thermoplastics or

            thermosetting resins

          - Abrasives with resin and diamond cold-out

          - Polyester resin polishers

          - Type 5 Extra pressed polishers

          - Putty and epoxy or polyester stucco
