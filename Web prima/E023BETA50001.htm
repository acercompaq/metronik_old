BETA 5000

 

Technical features                                                                              Press dimensions

Compress air stoking.                                                                             Width: 600 mm.                    

Power 1700 Kg a 7 Bar.                                                                          Depth: 670 mm.

Levels needed: 450 x 250 mm.                                                                Height: 800 mm.

Vertical light when the machine is open: 110 mm.

Piston stroke: 125 mm.                                                                           Controls board dimensions

Piston diameter: N.2 Diam.125 mm.                                                         Width: 490 mm.

Piston rod: 200 mm.                                                                               Depth: 450 mm.

Maximum pressure: 12 Bar.                                                                     Height: 190 mm.

Heating power levels: 5Kw.                                                                      Net weight: 250 Kg.

 

                                                                                                           

The press is made like a swan package with a mobile level guided by pneumatics cylinders;

the cylinders are set in the superior part of the press.

The press operates by movement from the high to the low. The stationary level is the inferior.

The working levels are smooth. The structure is made in welded steel. The electric controls are places

over a panel in a comfortable position for the operator. The machine lets you to have a cold and a

hot pressure cyclic, it also has manuals and pneumatic cycles.

The level temperature is completely independent and it's controlled by electronic thermocouple thermoregulator.
