Orbital mixer MK-OR33

Dimensions:

Width: 900 mm.

Depth: 530 mm.

Height: 2300 mm.

Weight: 320 Kg.

 

Technical specifications

This machine is characterized by a rotating orrery head on which one has fixed an adjustable side scraper and by two rotating satellite vanes which mix the product.

The orrery head and  the two satellite vanes are self-rotating.

Engine power spindle: 0.75 KW, Engine: three-phase AC asynchronous motor; Reduction gear: 1:2.42

Engine for rotation head 0.25 KW : three-phase AC asynchronous motor

Power vacuum pump : 0.75 KW

Rotation speed of orrery head from 0 to 65 revolutions per minutes

Spindle speed from 0 to 1240 revolutions per minutes

Vertical movement by pneumatic cylinders

Vertical stroke: 350 mm

Mixture container diam: 230 mm., height 230 mm.

Mixing container capacity: 7 lt.

Mixing capacity: 5 lt.

The vertical movement group is mounted on bronze bushings.

A control panel contains all the electrical instruments and controls
