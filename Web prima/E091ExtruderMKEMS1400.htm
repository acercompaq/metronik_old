Extruder MK-EMS1400

Dimensions

Width: 1800 mm.

Depth: 1300 mm.

Height: 4950 mm.

Weight: 1800 Kg.

 

Technical specifications

Extrusion power: 40 tons.

Extruder piston vertical stroke: 1600 mm.

Hydraulic engine: 10 HP.

Mixing cylinder diameter: 850 mm.

Mixing cylinder height:: 1300 mm.

The press has controls for automatic down-stroke and upstroke.

A control panel includes all the electrical instruments and controls.

The putty extruder is basically a press housing the extrusion cylinder.

On the rod of the main cylinder there is a piston which, driven by

the main cylinder of the press, compresses the putty helping its

extrusion through the ball valve on the mixture tank.

It's also possible to extrude the mixture by means of compressed air.

 

 In short: it allows for direct extrusion by piston, indirect extrusion by compressed air.
