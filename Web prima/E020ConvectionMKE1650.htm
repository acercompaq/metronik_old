Convection stove MK-E1650

 

 

Technical specifications

Usable width: 1000 mm.

Usable height: 1500 mm.

Usable depth: 1100 mm.

Working temperature: 250°C.

Heating by fan resistors.

Installed power: 16 kW.

Three-phase feed: 380 V, 50 Hz.

Horizontal air recirculation by radial sirocco fans,

3-HP engine.

Fibre insulation.

The stove has a chimney with a throttle valve for the

scavenging of fumes caused by the treatment.

The stove has a fresh air inlet equipped with a throttle valve.

Temperature control is ensured by a thermocouple electronic

thermoregulator.

Weight: 1500 Kg.

 

Options

Gas heating.

Load and dump trailers for the treated materials.

Electronic microprocessor programmer for thermal treatment cycle.
