Convection stove MK-E1000

Technical specifications

Usable width: 1000 mm.

Usable height: 1000 mm.

Usable depth: 1100 mm.

Working temperature: 250°C.

Heating by finned resistors.

Installed power: 12 kW.

Three-phase power supply: 380 V, 50 Hz.

Horizontal air recirculation by radial sirocco fans,

3-HP engine.

Fibre insulation.

The stove has a chimney with a throttle valve for the

scavenging of any fumes caused by the treatment.

The stove has a fresh air inlet equipped with a

throttle valve.

Temperature control is ensured by a thermocouple

electronic thermoregulator.

Gas heating.

Load and dump trailers for treated materials.

Electronic microprocessor programmer for thermal treatment cycle.
