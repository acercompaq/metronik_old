Wire treatment system MK-5/2U

Technical specifications

No.1 Ultrasound washing system consisting of a

double basin in AISI 304 stainless steel;

each basin measures 600 x 600 x 600 mm.

The first basin contains a resistor for the heating

of the detergent liquid and a plunger ultrasound

transducer consisting of 20 working transducers at

a frequency of 25 kHz (applied on the bottom of

the basin).

Also the second basin contains a resistor for the heating

of the detergent liquid used for the rinsing of the pieces treated.

On top of both basins there is a water coil for the condensation

of any vapours resulting from the evaporation of the washing liquid.

The temperature of the two basins is controlled by means of two

thermocouple electronic thermoregulators.

The electric controls are located in a special control panel.

The high-frequency feeder for the control of the ultrasound

transducers is located in a separate panel.

Both panels are placed on a support near the basins.

 

In both basins there are two motor-driven revolving rollers regulating the slow revolution of the

wire-carrier baskets.

 

No.1 Unwinding-rewinding unit consisting of two independent reel-carrier shafts.

The unwinding reel is equipped with a brake. The winding reel is controlled manually.

 

No.1 Resin-coating basin (for plastic materials) in stainless steel.

Said unit consists of a basin in which the resin is placed and by a vertical movement unit.

The vertical movement unit consists of a saddle equipped with ball-recirculation bearings;

the vertical movement is possible thanks to a pneumatic cylinder (one might opt for an

electric engine but this, obviously, at a higher price).

On the vertical saddle there is an engine-driven reel-carrier shaft allowing for the revolution of the shaft itself.

The resin is recycled by means of a pump with stainless steel cogs with a by-pass valve.

A three-way tap allows the basin to be emptied.

 

No.1 Resin-coating basin (for rubber) in stainless steel.

Said unit consists of a basin containing the resin and a vertical movement unit.

The vertical movement unit consists of a saddle equipped with ball-recirculation bearings;

the vertical movement is possible thanks to a pneumatic cylinder (possibly by an electric engine but at a

higher price).

On the vertical saddle there is an engine-driven reel-carrier shaft allowing the revolution

of the shaft itself.

The resin is recycled by means of a pump with stainless steel cogs with a by-pass valve.

A three-way tap allows the basin to be emptied.

 

No. 1 Cold-air drying unit. The unit consists of basin in which there is inserted a reel-carrier shaft.

The reel-carrier shaft is made to slowly revolve by means of a gearmotor with a worm screw.

The drying air is produced by a low-pressure impeller with sirocco blades.

 

No. 2 Parallel-track traverse systems mounted over the basins.

Said tracks allow for the application of lifting organs. (Said organs will be quoted separately).

 

No. 1 Hood (without aspirator) placed on the load tracks makes it possible to evacuate any residual fumes.

 

No. 2 Chain pulleys to effortlessly move the carriers from one treatment basin to the other.

 

All this is mounted on an iron base with wheels. All the controls are located on a single control panel.
