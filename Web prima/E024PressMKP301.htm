Press for thermosetting resins MK-P30/1

Rating plate data

Power: 30 tons.

Usable surfaces: 420 x 420 mm. in C40 steel or aluminium.

Vertical port when machine is open: 350 mm.

Piston stroke: 350 mm.

Bore piston: 140 mm.

Max. pressure: 260 bar.

Gearcase motor: 5 kW.

Surface heating power: 12/18 kW (to be agreed upon).

Magnesium oxide resistors.

Surface temperature: 150 °C.

Surface cooling by means of throwaway water.

This four-column press is equipped with a mobile surface

mounted on bronze sliding bearings; the cylinder is located in

the back of the press.

The press functions with a downward movement.

The fixed surface is the lower one. The support surfaces are smooth.

The structure is in electrowelded steel.

The pump is of the high-pressure cog type.

The hydraulic circuit consists of electrovalves letting down and pulling

up the upper and intermediate mobile surfaces.

 

Pressure control is ensured by means of direct control maximum valve.

A pressure switch ensure pressure setting and stops the pump.

The electric system is housed in a special control panel; the most frequently used controls are placed so

as to be allow for easy operator- access. The machine allows for hot and cold pressing cycles.

A safety barrier is placed at the entry of the press on the operator side.

Surface temperature is controlled by means of thermocouple electronic thermoregulator.

 

Controls and working mode

The machine allows for a totally automatic working cycle.
