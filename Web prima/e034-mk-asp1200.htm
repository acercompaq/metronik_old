Powder suction booth MK-ASP1200

Overall dimensions:

Length: 1800 mm.

Depth: 900 mm.

Height: 2850 mm.

Hood Weight: 400 Kg.

 

Technical Characteristics:

Working Length: 1200 mm.

Working Depth: 900 mm.

Design Suction Flow: from  1400 to 2800 m3/h

Nominal Flow: 1600 m3/h

Suction Pressure at Nominal Flow: 1600 m3/h

Aspirator Motor Power: 3 HP (2.2 Kw)

Filter type PEPE 504

The system can be operated manually.

The booth is made of varnished all-welded steel and it is supplied with lateral and bottom powder suction.

A cylindrical filter retains the powders and conveys them to a container to be collected. A butterfly valve is mounted at the end of the filter to adjust the intake air. 

The system is supplied with four supporting feet for the vertical adjustment of the working surface.

 

The booth is supplied with neonlight illumination.

The booth is depressurized.

 

The air exhaust outlet of the system must be placed outside the working environment.
