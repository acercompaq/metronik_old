BETA 2000

Press dimensions:

Width: 300 mm.

Depth: 520 mm.

Height: 630 mm.

 

Controls board dimensions:

Width: 490 mm.

Depth: 450 mm.

Height: 190 mm.

Net weight: 70 Kg.

 

Technical features:

Compress air stoking.

Power 350 Kg a 7 Bar.

Levels needed: 130 x 165 mm.

Vertical light when the machine is open: 70 mm.

Piston stroke: 125 mm.

Piston diameter: N.2 Diam.80 mm.

Piston rod: 25 mm.

Maximum pressure: 12 Bar.

Heating power levels: 1300 Watt.

 

The press is made like a swan package with a mobile level guided by pneumatics cylinders; the cylinders are set in the superior part of the press.

The press operates by movement from the high to the low. The stationary level is the inferior.

The working levels are smooth. The structure is made in welded steel. The electric controls are places over a panel in a comfortable position for the operator. The machine lets you to have a cold and a hot pressure cycle, it also has manuals and pneumatic cycles.

The level temperature is completely independent and it's controlled by electronic thermocouple thermoregulator.
