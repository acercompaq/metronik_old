                  Weighing trailer MK-PE300

                  This trailer has been designed so as to allow for easy weighing of all the components

               that collect on the weighing surface. When the formula is completed, the components

               are carried over to the mixer into which they are tilted without having to be lifted.

 

                  Lifter MK-MU12

                  This lifter has been designed to be used with the MK-M20 mixer, and highlights its

               capacity for elevated production.

               The dolly is used to discharge the mixture from the mixer container and pour it directly

               into the plastic moulds placed on the tables.

               Besides the vertical movement, the dolly has rotary movement and a lateral exit arm

               movement.

               It is extremely easy to use and clean and definitely ensures saving of time in

               the casting phase if compared with traditional methods.

 

                  Mixer MK-S5600

                  This mixer has been expressly designed to supply - in the fastest and most rational of

               ways – the chloride magnesium and water mixture required for the production of

               magnesite abrasive tools.

               It is extremely easy to use and clean and allows for substantial saving of time when

               preparing the solution.

               The mixer produces the correct amount of solution for daily or weekly use.

               It allows for the sedimentation of any impurities on the bottom; said impurities are then

               removed through specific piping.

 

                  Marking machine-pattern draw machine MK-MS5

                  This machine sees to the marking and shakeout of magnesite abrasive tools.

              The group is particularly suitable owing to its capacity for production.

 

                  Orbital mixer MK-OR20

                  This machine is characterized by a rotating orrery head on which one has fixed an

               adjustable side scraper and by two rotating satellite vanes which mix the product.

               This mixer is expressly designed to be used in factories requiring large-scale production;

               it makes it possible to quickly mix large quantities of materials, homogeneously and

               without any lumps.

               It is particularly indicated for the production of mixtures with no or little

               liquid component

               such as: resinous abrasives, powdered resins, pigments, special refractories, chemical

               compounds, mix of press-printables, etc.

 

                   Orbital mixer MK-OR33

               This mixer is expressly designed to be used in factories requiring  high quality of the finished

               product; it  makes it possible to quickly mix resins with liquid compound homogeneously  without

               any lumps and air bubbles.

                   It is particularly indicated for the production of abrasive tools with liquid compound such as:

               epoxy, polyester, polyurethane.
