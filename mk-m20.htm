Mixer MK-M20

 

This mixer is expressly designed to be used in factories requiring large-scale production; it makes it possible to quickly mix large quantities of materials, homogeneously and  without any lumps.

 

It's particularly recommended to produce: magnesium and resinous abrasives, varnishes and resins, pigments, special refractories, chemical compounds, etc.
