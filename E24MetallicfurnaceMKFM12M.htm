Metallic muffle furnace MK-FM12M

Dimensions             

Width: 520 mm.

Depth: 650 mm.

Height: 650 mm.

Weight: 60 Kg.

           

Technical specifications

Usable width: 180 mm.

Usable height: 130mm.

Usable depth: 400 mm.

Working temperature: 1200° C.

Heating by Kanthal resistors.

Installed power: 4 kW.

Monophase power supply:220 V, 50 Hz.

Isolation in ceramic fibre.

The furnace has a chimney with a burner for

the combustion of exhaust gases.

 

Temperature control is ensured by a thermocouple electronic thermoregulator.

A particular safety system prevents the operator from performing dangerous operations.

 

Options

Electronic microprocessor programmer for thermal treatment cycle.

Support trailer for the pieces dumped from the furnace.
