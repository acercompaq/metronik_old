Mixer MK-MMS1400

Our mixer has been designed to allow for fast mixing of resinous products.

Particularly the mixer has been designed to produce large quantities of stucco and putty for marble.

It's characterized by ease and flexibility of use with the automatic extrusion group.

 

Extruder MK-EMS1400

This extruder has been designed to ensure easy extrusion and glassing of fluid and thick masses.

In particular the extruder has been designed to produce large quantities of stucco and putty for marble.

It's characterized by facility and flexibility of use with the automatic weighing group.

 

Deaerator MK-VMS1400

This deaerator has been designed and developed to allow for the quick extraction of air bubbles from the most sundries resins products.

Above all this deaerator has been designed to produce large amounts of stucco and putty for marble.

It is extremely easy to use along with the automatic extruder unit.
